Define Problem Statement

 The various information that are needed for the international students, when they join RIT, are found in different places but they are not well structured. There is no single place or single platform to gather the information that is required by the international students.

Identify Target Audience

mdi-account-group-outline The target audience for our project are the international students at RIT, both new and existing. mdi-check-circle-outline

Propose Solution

 The main idea of this project is to develop an application to gather all the information that are required for incoming international students and then organize it and help the students in a step by step manner.
