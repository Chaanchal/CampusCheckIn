Problem Statement

The various information available to students of RIT are not well structured and delivered effectively. There is no single platform to gather this information required by the students and this leads to the student been overwhelmed and usually confused or miss important steps that require their attention.

Direction of a solution

  • Identify and gather information required by the students
  • Present information in a structured manner
    • Present the information in a card-based interface
    • Categorize information into relevant groups
    • Keep a check on the progress of checklist items
  • Provide notification through push notification, text messages, and emails for effective communication
